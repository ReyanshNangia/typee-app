Everything in one quiet window

🪶

Floating & instant

Summon Typee from any app with a double-tap of Control, Option, or Command.

🗂

Multi-note carousel

Swipe or use ⌘[ / ⌘] to move between notes. Drag the dots to reorder.

✍️

Rich text

Bold, underline, and per-selection font sizing with ⌘= and ⌘−.

🔢

Smart lists

Type 1., a., or i. then space for auto-continuing numbered, lettered, or roman lists.

🎨

Color themes

Tint any note from a warm palette — even the scrollbar matches.

🔁

Rock-solid hotkey

Survives sleep and wake, with Accessibility permission that sticks.

Up and running in a minute

  1. Download the DMG and drag Typee onto Applications.
  2. First launch: right-click Typee → Open (a one-time macOS step for free, un-notarized apps).
  3. Grant Accessibility when asked, so the global hotkey works.
